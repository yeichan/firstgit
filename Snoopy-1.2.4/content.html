■ Question Summarize the points made in the lecture you just heard, explaining how they cast doubt on the points made in the reading


▶ Topic : Some people prefer to live in a big city. Others prefer to live in a small town. Which do you prefer?

▶ Your Answer : Some people prefer to live in a small city while others prefer to live in a big city. However, as far as I am concerned, I prefer to live in a big city since we can broaden our cultural view and big cities have a good educational environment. I will briefly describe the reasons for this.

 

To begin with, living in big cities make our cultural view more widely than living in small cities because there are a lot of facilities and activities that offer many events and exhibitions in which i can take part in. Let's take a case of Seoul, the biggest city in Korea and 12th populated city in the world. The city has multinational restaurants such as indian, italian, japanese and so on. For these reasons, people who live in seoul always can taste a lot of foods that come from another countries. Not only that, there are many facilities to access another culture, so we do not have to worry about the way to learn about another culture. For instance, If you have an assignment to inspect about culture in your universitiy, you can easily go to national museum in Seoul. In that point, living in big cities are more valuable to broad our cultural view.

 

Second, The big cities provide good educational environment so it gives more opportunities for students who live in big cities than those who do not. Moreover, it is easy to access to a lot of facilities and opportunities that give them to satisfy intellectual demand. I will take Seoul for example. Seoul has many educational faciliites such as libraries, many universities and various kinds of bookstores so people can gain a lot of helpful things by visiting and experiencing that place. Also there are many companies so college students have oppurtunities to apply for an internship.According to recent research by Korea times, people who have lived only big cities are more likely to have high possiblities to enter the prestigious university than those who have lived only small cities.

 

As I mentioned above, I strongly believe that living in a big city is better than living in a small city. This is because, big cities is not only helpful to broaden our cultural view but also it gives us a lot of opportunities for education.
